Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Introduces young learners to basic literacy, numeracy, and social skills through interactive and play-based activities.

Elementary School

Builds foundational knowledge in subjects like math, science, language arts, and social studies while fostering critical thinking and creativity.

Middle (10-16) Years

Prepares students for higher education by deepening subject knowledge, promoting independent learning, and developing problem-solving skills.
